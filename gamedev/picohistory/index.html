Picohistory

A PICO-8 reimagining

The idea here was to take a few old games and rebuild them, with modifications, for the PICO-8 fantasy console. I ended up with four; here they are for your entertainment.

  • Pong
  • Spacewar!
  • Pac-Man
  • Hunt the Wumpus

This was an interesting project; I never really ran into the PICO-8's limitations, but working in Lua, and building most of several games from the ground up, was educational. The results are more entertaining than I expected of such old things, too.

I did use a chunk of engine code from Under Construction (by Glip and Eevee) in Pong, but the later ones stripped out most of it in favor of something I understood better by way of building it myself. I think that was the right choice for rapid development, but none of the stuff I built really lends itself to a re-usable engine, so who knows.
